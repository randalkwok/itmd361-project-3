Magic

How did you get into magic?

Since I was little, I was always interested in magic. Whether it was watching David Blaine on TV or trying to learn simple card tricks on YouTube, I found it fascinating. However, I never really got into magic seriously until the beginning of 2017 when I showed my family a simple card trick I learned on YouTube. Their reaction and just the fun we had made me want to just learn more and more. I have not skipped a day without practicing and learning since.

Why are you interested in magic?

I don't do magic because I want to fool people. I see magic as a way to connect to people and bring joy to their day. Magic allows me to for just a few moments show people what shouldn't be possible. When we were kids, the smallest things would blow our minds. Everything new was amazing. That's the feeling I want people to feel again. That's why I love magic so much.

What kind of magic do you do?

I mainly do close up card magic.
