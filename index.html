Why computer science?

Computer programming really excites me. I’ve grown up during the time computers are rapidly occupying our world. I’ve always wondered how they work and that’s why I took a computer science class in high school. In this class, I learned programming isn’t just 1’s and 0’s, but a completely new way of thinking and solving problems. Just like you need different mindsets to solve either math or science problems, you need a new mindset to solve computer science problems and that’s what excites me.

It’s a mixture of analyzing the problem, assessing the tools you have, and staying organized. Being able to complete a computational problem is more satisfying than any other math or science problem. It made me realize just how complex some software is. I look at computers differently in that I always wonder how a certain program might work.
